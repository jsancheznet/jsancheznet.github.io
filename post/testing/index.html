A short personal story

Ever since i can remember i have been fascinated by computers. When i was a little kid maybe 5 or 6 years old my father had a retail shop that sold and repaired electronics. One night i heard that a computer wizard was coming to setup the servers and the network of the shop. I stayed up late to watch this guy work. At that time it was common in personal computers to have a turbo button so you could change the clock speed of the pc and usually there was a little display in front of the computer that displayed the speed the computer was currently at. That night i wanted to see the top speed of the new server, so i walked towards it and pushed the turbo button. Wrong Button! i accidentaly reseted the server while some work was being done on it. The guy was not happy, my father scolded me and told me to go to sleep. That night something changed, i wanted to learn to do the same thing as this guy.
